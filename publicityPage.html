MANAGER

Chairman Changqing Li

Founder and Chairman of Big Data Service Co., Ltd., is a pioneer of data technology in China. Changqing began his career in a leading domestic data company and has committed to data application in financial industry for almost two decades.

He served as Manager of Terminal Department to take charge of terminal product development of Juyuan Data Co., Ltd. between 2002 and 2008. He then became Chief Architect of GAO Time Co., Ltd. and led a number of data center projects serving financial market.

In October 2011, Changqing established Big Data Co., Ltd. in Shanghai and became chairman of the company. He is a member of the board of directors as well.
